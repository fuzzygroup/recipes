# Ingredients

* 1 cup Olive or Grapeseed Oil
* 1/3 cup Balsamic Vinegar
* 2 tbsp Honey
* 1/4 cup Water
* 3 cloves Minced Garlic

# Steps:

1.  In a salad dressing container combine all ingredients.
2.  Shake.  
3.  Enjoy.
