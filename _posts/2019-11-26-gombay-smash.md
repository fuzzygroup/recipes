## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 oz Coconut Rum
* 3/4 oz White Rum
* Splash of Galliano
* Pineapple Juice

## Steps

1.  In a short cocktail glass, add 3 or 4 ice cubes.
2.  Add both rums and Galliano.
3.  Top with Pineapple Juice.
