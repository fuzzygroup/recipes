# Ingredients

* 1 can frozen lemonade

* 1 empty lemonade can coconut rum

* frozen peaches

* ice as desired

# Steps

1.  Combine all ingredients in blender

2.  Whizz together.  Adjust ice as desired for freezy-ness level.
