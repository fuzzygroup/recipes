# Ingredients
* 1 bottle Moscato, Proseco, Asti or another sparkling wine
* 1/2 cup Apple Cider
* 1/3 cup brandy
* 1/2 cup butter shots

# Steps

In a large pitcher combine all the ingredients.  Taste and adjust amount of apple cider if it feels "too boozy".
