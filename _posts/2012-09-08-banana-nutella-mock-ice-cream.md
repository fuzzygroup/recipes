# Ingredients

* 4 to 6	very ripe bananas, frozen
* 3	tbsp nutella

# Steps

1.  Take frozen bananas and dice them into rounds.
2.  Place in food processor and puree.
3.  Add nutella and run thru food processor until smooth.
