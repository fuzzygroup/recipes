# Ingredients

* 2 bottles Moscato or Proseco

* 1 cup apricot brandy

* 1 cup coconut rum

* 2 fresh peaches sliced

* 16 oz strawberries

* 1/2 fresh pineapple

* 6 oz raspberries

# Steps

1.  Combine all.  Shake.

2.  Refrigerate and serve.
