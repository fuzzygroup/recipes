# Ingredients

* 1 bottle Moscato, Proseco, Asti or another sparkling wine
* 1/2 cup apple cider
* 1 apple, cored and diced
* 1/3 cup brandy
* 1/2 cup butter shots

# Steps

1.  In a large pitcher combine all ingredients.
2.  Enjoy!
