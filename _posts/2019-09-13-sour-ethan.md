# Ingredients

 * 1 shot Amaretto
 * 1/2 shot whipped cream vodka
 * 1/2 shot Limoncello
 * Lemonade - top with
 * Prosecco 

# Steps

1. Fill a tall glass with ice. 
2. Add the Amaretto, Whipped Cream Vodka and Lemoncello
3. Add 1 shot Prosecco 
4. Top with Lemonade
